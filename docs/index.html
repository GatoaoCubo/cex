What is CEXAI

Typed knowledge infrastructure for LLM agents

Most AI agents are a system prompt plus a few tools. CEXAI treats AI as typed infrastructure: every piece of knowledge is a kind, every kind has a builder, every builder follows the 8-function pipeline, and seven sin-driven nuclei collaborate through a governance layer that compounds over time.

Intelligence compounds faster when exchanged.

Composable

8F × 12 pillars × 300 kinds = the factory floor. Spawn a nucleus in minutes.

Sovereign

Self-assimilating

Every conversation compiles into typed, governed, searchable knowledge assets.
