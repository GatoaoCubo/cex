# Email Sequence Templates — Marketing Campaigns

## 1. Welcome Sequence (5-email series)

### Email 1: Welcome & Expectation Setting
**Send**: Immediately after signup  
**Subject**: Welcome to [BRAND] - here's what happens next  

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Welcome to [BRAND_NAME]! I'm [SENDER_NAME], and I'm excited to help you [MAIN_BENEFIT].

Here's what you can expect over the next few days:

→ Tomorrow: [SPECIFIC_VALUE_1]
→ Day 3: [SPECIFIC_VALUE_2] 
→ Day 5: [SPECIFIC_VALUE_3]

But first, can you do me a quick favor? Hit reply and tell me:
What's your biggest challenge with [PROBLEM_AREA] right now?

I read every response and often create content based on what I hear.

Talk soon,
[SIGNATURE]

P.S. Make sure to add my email to your contacts so my messages don't end up in spam!
```

```
1. [STEP_1]

3. [STEP_3]

Try it out and let me know how it goes!

Best,
[SIGNATURE]

P.S. Tomorrow I'll share the [NEXT_VALUE_PROMISE] - keep an eye out!
```

## 2. Product Launch Sequence (6-email series)

### Pre-Launch Email 1: Announcement
**Subject**: Something big is coming...

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

I've been working on something for the past [TIMEFRAME] that I think you're going to love.

It's called [PRODUCT_NAME], and it's designed to help you [MAIN_BENEFIT].

Here's a sneak peek: [TEASER_DETAILS]

I'll be sharing more details over the next few days, including:
• What exactly it includes
• How it works  
• Real results from beta users
• Special pricing for early access

Keep an eye on your inbox - launch day is [DATE]!

[SIGNATURE]
```

### Pre-Launch Email 2: Behind the Scenes
**Subject**: Why I created [PRODUCT_NAME]

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

Yesterday I told you about [PRODUCT_NAME] - my new [PRODUCT_TYPE].

Today I want to share WHY I created it.

[ORIGIN_STORY - problem you observed, gap in market, customer requests]

After helping [NUMBER] people with [PROBLEM_AREA], I kept hearing the same challenges:
• [COMMON_CHALLENGE_1]
• [COMMON_CHALLENGE_2]  
• [COMMON_CHALLENGE_3]

That's when I realized: people need [SOLUTION_APPROACH].

So I spent [TIMEFRAME] creating exactly that.

[PRODUCT_NAME] includes:
→ [COMPONENT_1]
→ [COMPONENT_2]  
→ [COMPONENT_3]

Launch day is [DATE] - I can't wait to share it with you!

[SIGNATURE]
```

## 3. Re-engagement Sequence (3-email series)

### Email 1: "We miss you"
**Subject**: Did I do something wrong?

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

I noticed you haven't opened my emails in a while.

Did I do something wrong?

Maybe I've been sending too many emails? Or perhaps the content isn't relevant to where you are right now?

I'd love to make this better.

Can you take 30 seconds to let me know:
1. What would make these emails more valuable for you?
2. How often would you like to hear from me?
3. What topics interest you most?

[Quick feedback form: LINK]

Or just hit reply and let me know.

I read every response.

[SIGNATURE]
```

### Email 2: "Last attempt" 
**Subject**: Last email (unless you want me to stay)

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

This is probably my last email to you.

I hate to see you go, but I respect that your inbox is sacred space.

Before I remove you from my list, I wanted to share one last thing:

[VALUABLE_RESOURCE or SPECIAL_OFFER]

If you found value in this, [STAY_SUBSCRIBED_LINK].

If not, no hard feelings - you'll be automatically removed after [TIMEFRAME].

Either way, I wish you all the best with [THEIR_GOALS]!

[SIGNATURE]
```

### Email 3: "Goodbye" (with win-back offer)
**Subject**: Goodbye (and thank you)

```
Hi [FIRST_NAME],

This is it - my final email to you.

Thank you for letting me be part of your journey, even briefly.

As a parting gift, here's [FINAL_VALUABLE_RESOURCE] - no strings attached.

[RESOURCE_LINK]

If you ever change your mind, you can resubscribe anytime at [RESUBSCRIBE_LINK].

Until then, wishing you massive success!

[SIGNATURE]

P.S. If you know someone who might benefit from [MAIN_VALUE_PROPOSITION], feel free to send them to [SIGNUP_LINK].
```

## Usage Guidelines

### Personalization Variables
```yaml
required_variables:
  - FIRST_NAME
  - BRAND_NAME  
  - MAIN_BENEFIT
  - SENDER_NAME
  
optional_variables:
  - COMPANY_NAME
  - LAST_PURCHASE
  - SIGNUP_SOURCE
  - ENGAGEMENT_LEVEL
```

### Timing Recommendations
```yaml
welcome_sequence:
  email_1: "0 hours (immediately)"
  email_2: "24 hours"  
  email_3: "48 hours"
  email_4: "72 hours"
  email_5: "96 hours"
  
launch_sequence:
  pre_1: "7 days before launch"
  pre_2: "3 days before launch"
  launch: "Launch day"
  reminder_1: "24 hours later"
  reminder_2: "48 hours later"  
  final: "72 hours later"
```

### A/B Testing Recommendations
```yaml
test_elements:
  subject_lines:
    - "Personalized vs generic"
    - "Question vs statement" 
    - "Benefit vs curiosity"
    
  call_to_actions:
    - "Action-specific vs benefit-focused"
    - "Button vs text link"
    - "Urgency vs no urgency"
    
  email_length:
    - "Short (100-200 words) vs long (300-500 words)"
    - "Single focus vs multiple points"
```
