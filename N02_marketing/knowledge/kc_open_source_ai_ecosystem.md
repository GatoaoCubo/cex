# Open Source AI Ecosystem 2026

The open source AI ecosystem in 2026 is characterized by rapid innovation and diverse platforms. Key players include:

## Major Platforms

1. **Ollama**  
   - Focus: Lightweight, easy-to-deploy models  
   - Hosting: Cloud (Ollama Cloud), Self-hosted (Ollama Server)  
   - Pricing: Free tier with limited models; paid plans for enterprise access

2. **vLLM**  
   - Focus: High-throughput inference optimization  
   - Hosting: Self-hosted (via Docker), Cloud (integrations with AWS/GCP)  
   - Pricing: Open source with optional commercial support

3. **llama.cpp**  
   - Focus: Portable, efficient model execution  
   - Hosting: Self-hosted (Linux/macOS), WebAssembly (WASM)  
   - Pricing: Completely free and open source

4. **Hugging Face**  
   - Focus: Model sharing and collaboration  
   - Hosting: Cloud (Inference API), Self-hosted (Docker)  
   - Pricing: Free tier with 100k tokens/month; paid plans for higher limits

5. **Together.ai**  
   - Focus: Enterprise-grade model hosting  
   - Hosting: Cloud (Together API), Self-hosted (custom deployments)  
   - Pricing: Free tier with 100k tokens/month; paid plans for business use

6. **Groq**  
   - Focus: Low-latency, high-throughput inference  
   - Hosting: Cloud (Groq API), Self-hosted (experimental)  
   - Pricing: Free tier with 100k tokens/month; paid plans for production use
