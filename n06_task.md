The user journey map documents the end-to-end customer experience from initial awareness to final conversion. Key stages include:

1. **Awareness**  
   - User discovers brand through organic search, social media, or referrals  
   - Content marketing (blogs, videos) and influencer partnerships drive initial interest  

2. **Consideration**  
   - Comparative research via reviews, comparisons, and demos  
   - Email campaigns and targeted ads nurture decision-making  

3. **Decision**  
   - Final purchase through website, app, or customer service channels  
   - Trust signals like security badges and customer testimonials influence choice  

4. **Post-Purchase**  
   - Onboarding process and product setup instructions  
   - Ongoing support via help desks, FAQs, and community forums  

This map helps align marketing strategies with customer behavior patterns while identifying optimization opportunities across touchpoints.
