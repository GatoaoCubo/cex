## Summary
Prototype testing in the Edison Engineering Lab identified a critical wiring fault delaying the project. Despite the setback, the experience enhanced future fault diagnosis processes.

## Pattern
- Implement comprehensive diagnostic checks before initial testing.
- Schedule incremental reviews during prototype development stages.
- Improve communication of test results across teams promptly.

## Anti-Pattern
- Overlooked initial wiring check led to a major project delay.
- Inconsistent reporting of test outcomes across departments caused confusion.

## Context
- Environment: Edison Engineering Lab, prototype testing setup.
- Satellite: engineering_lab_satellite
- Timing: Conducted in September 2023 during the project evaluation phase.
- Constraints: Limited component accessibility delayed diagnostics.

## Impact
- Major wiring fault identified, causing project delays.
- Enhanced future diagnostic strategies, reducing similar future delays.

## Reproducibility
- Conditions: Extensive diagnostic protocols must be established for similar projects.
- Confidence: MEDIUM
- Caveats: Varies with project scope and component accessibility.
