# Content Strategy Index

Estrategia de conteudo GATO3 para Instagram + Blog. Horizonte Q2 2026 (Apr 15 -- Jul 15).

## Numeros-Chave

| Metrica | Alvo |
|---------|------|
| Posts Instagram / semana | 5 (3 feed + 2 reels/carrossel) |
| Blog posts / semana | 2 |
| Total 13 semanas | ~70 IG + ~26 blog = ~96 pecas |
| Engagement rate alvo | 3-5% |
| Quality score minimo | 4.0/5.0 ([[Content_Quality_Rubric]]) |
