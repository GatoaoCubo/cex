**Question**:  
What is the process for submitting a support request?

**Canonical Answer**:  
1. Navigate to the "Help Center" section on our website.  
2. Select "Contact Support" from the main menu.  
3. Choose the appropriate support category (e.g., billing, technical issues).  
4. Fill out the required fields in the contact form.  
5. Submit your request and await a response within 24 business hours.
