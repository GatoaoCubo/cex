# Account Expansion Play

## Upsell Triggers
- Subscription anniversary milestones
- Usage thresholds (e.g., 80% capacity)
- Feature adoption benchmarks
- Competitor pricing signals
- Account health score alerts

## Cross-Sell Map
| Product | Trigger | Channel | Success Metric |
|---------|---------|---------|----------------|
| Analytics | Usage report | Email | Feature adoption |
| Support | SLA breach | In-app | Resolution time |
| Training | Onboarding delay | Webinar | Completion rate |

## NRR Mechanics
- **Net Revenue Retention** = (Current Revenue - Churn Revenue) / Previous Revenue
- Focus on:
  - Contract term renewals
  - Add-on purchases
  - Upgraded service tiers
  - Success story referrals

## AE Talk Track
1. **Acknowledge value**: "Your team's adoption of X has driven 30% growth"
2. **Identify gaps**: "We notice you haven't utilized Y - would you like to explore?"
3. **Present options**: "Here are 3 ways to expand your value with our solutions"
4. **Close with action**: "Let's schedule a demo to implement this next step"
