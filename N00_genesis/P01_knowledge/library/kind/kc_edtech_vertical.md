**Education/EdTech Industry Vertical Overview**

This knowledge card covers key aspects of the education technology (EdTech) industry vertical, including regulatory frameworks, integration standards, and data privacy considerations.

**Key Topics:**

1. **FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act)**
   - U.S. law protecting student education records
   - Requires schools to obtain parental consent before disclosing student data
   - Applies to all schools receiving federal funding

2. **COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)**
   - U.S. law governing online data collection from children under 13
   - Requires parental consent for data collection
   - Mandates clear privacy policies for websites targeting children

3. **LMS Integration (LTI - Learning Tools Interoperability)**
   - Standard for connecting learning management systems with external tools
   - Enables seamless integration between platforms like Canvas, Moodle, and third-party apps
   - Uses OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication

4. **Student Data Privacy**
   - Critical for EdTech platforms handling sensitive information
   - Requires encryption of data at rest and in transit
   - Mandates access controls and audit trails

5. **Use Cases**
   - Virtual classrooms and remote learning platforms
   - Student information systems (SIS)
   - Educational analytics and learning management systems
   - Compliance management tools for schools

**Industry Challenges:**
- Balancing data utility with privacy protection
- Navigating complex regulatory landscapes
- Ensuring secure integration across multiple platforms
- Maintaining compliance while delivering innovative educational solutions
