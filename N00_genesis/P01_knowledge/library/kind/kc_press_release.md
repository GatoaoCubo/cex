# Press Release Guide

## AP Style Elements
- **Headline**: Bold, concise, and attention-grabbing (e.g., "Local Tech Startup Secures $5M in Funding")
- **Dateline**: "City, State — Date" (e.g., "San Francisco, CA — April 5, 2024")
- **Lede**: First paragraph summarizing key facts: who, what, when, where, why, how
- **Quotes**: Direct quotes from executives/officials (e.g., "We're thrilled to announce this milestone," said CEO Jane Doe)
- **Boilerplate**: Company overview and mission statement
- **Contacts**: Media contact information with phone number, email, and website

## Structure Template
```
[Headline]
[Dateline]

[Lead paragraph with key details]

[Quote from executive]

[Additional context/technical details]

[Boilerplate]

[Contact information]
```

## Example
**LOCAL TECH STARTUP SECURES $5M IN FUNDING**  
San Francisco, CA — April 5, 2024  

Local tech startup InnovateX has raised $5 million in Series A funding to expand its AI-powered analytics platform.  

"We're thrilled to announce this milestone," said CEO Jane Doe. "This investment will accelerate our mission to democratize data science."  

The funding comes from top-tier venture capital firms. The platform now serves over 100 enterprises.  

**About Acme AI**  
Acme AI is a technology company developing AI tools for enterprise data analysis. Founded in 2020, the company has grown to 50 employees and serves clients across 15 countries.  

**Media Contact**  
Press Team  
press@example.com  
(555) 555-0198  
www.example.com
```

## Tips
- Use active voice and present tense
- Avoid jargon; explain technical terms
- Include specific metrics and dates
- Maintain neutral, factual tone
- Include multimedia assets (photos, videos) when possible
```
