# Code of Conduct

Let's make sure we all get along and work well together! Here's what we're committed to:

## How We Want to Work
- Treat each other with respect and kindness
- Use language that includes everyone
- Give credit where it's due
- Help each other learn and grow

## What We Shouldn't Do
- Be mean or disrespectful
- Make fun of people based on their identity
- Spam or flood conversations
- Do things that stop others from contributing

## When Things Go Wrong
If you see or experience something that's not right:
1. Tell a project leader right away
2. Share what happened clearly and honestly
3. Ask for privacy if you want
4. We'll get back to you quickly

## What Happens Next
We'll handle issues carefully:
1. **Report**: Share your concerns with us
2. **Check**: We'll look into it thoroughly
3. **Fix**: Take action that fits the situation
4. **Appeal**: You can ask for a second look if needed

We believe in learning from mistakes. Our goal is to help everyone succeed while keeping the community safe and welcoming.
