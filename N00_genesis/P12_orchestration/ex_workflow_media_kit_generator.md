# Stages

1. Intake product or offer details.
2. Generate the copy hierarchy.
3. Request visual assets and usage notes.
4. Validate completeness.
5. Export for sales, partnerships, and creator use.

## Deliverables

- headline_set
- value_prop
- audience_fit
- talking_points
- asset_list
- usage_guidelines
