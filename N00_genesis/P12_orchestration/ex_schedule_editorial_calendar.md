# Weekly Rhythm

```yaml
monday:
  focus: educational
  asset: blog_or_carousel
tuesday:
  focus: community
  asset: story_or_short_post
wednesday:
  focus: product_or_offer
  asset: reel_or_demo
thursday:
  focus: objection_handling
  asset: carousel_or_thread
friday:
  focus: roundup
  asset: feed_post
```

## Planning Rules

- Every week must cover at least two content themes.
- No more than two consecutive sales-led pieces.
- Each blog topic should feed at least one social derivative.
