description: |
  A customer segment is a group of customers with similar characteristics, needs, and behaviors. This artifact defines the criteria for segmenting customers, including firmographics (industry, company size, location) and needs (pain points, goals). Key attributes include:
  
  1. **Industry**: Sector or vertical (e.g., healthcare, tech)
  2. **Company Size**: Revenue, employees, or market cap
  3. **Location**: Geographical regions or countries
  4. **Pain Points**: Specific challenges or problems the segment faces
  5. **Goals**: Objectives the segment aims to achieve
  6. **Buying Behavior**: How the segment makes purchasing decisions
  7. **Decision-Makers**: Key stakeholders involved in the buying process
  
  This definition helps in creating targeted marketing strategies and product offerings.
