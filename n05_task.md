description: |
  Customer segment is a classification of buyers based on shared characteristics that influence their purchasing behavior. This definition artifact includes:
  
  **Firmographics** (business attributes):
  - Industry vertical (e.g., SaaS, healthcare)
  - Company size (employee count, revenue range)
  - Geographic location (region, country)
  - Technological maturity (digital adoption level)
  - Organizational structure (centralized/decentralized)
  
  **Customer Needs** (psychographic and behavioral):
  - Primary pain points (time, cost, complexity)
  - Decision-making criteria (ROI, scalability, integration)
  - Buying journey stages (awareness, consideration, decision)
  - Preferred communication channels (email, LinkedIn, webinars)
  - Budget constraints and purchasing authority
  
  This segmentation framework enables precise targeting of Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) by aligning product value propositions with specific business requirements and operational contexts.
