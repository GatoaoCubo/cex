## Observation
Common issues include inconsistent visual hierarchy, unclear value differentiation between tiers, and weak CTAs that fail to drive conversions. Overloading pages with data often dilutes key messages.

## Pattern
Effective pricing pages use clear tiered layouts with aligned feature comparisons, bold CTAs, and concise copy that emphasizes benefits over technical specs. Visual cues (e.g., color, spacing) highlight premium tiers.

## Evidence
Reviewed artifacts showed 25% higher engagement when tiers were compared via side-by-side tables and CTAs were placed below pricing cards.

## Recommendations
- Prioritize visual hierarchy to distinguish tiers (e.g., size, color).
- Align feature comparisons directly with pricing tiers for clarity.
- Use action-oriented CTAs (e.g., "Start Free Trial") above fold.
- Test mobile responsiveness for tiered layouts and interactive elements.
- Avoid jargon; focus on outcomes (e.g., "Save 30%") over features.
