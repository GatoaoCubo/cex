## Crew Role  
Integrates healthcare-specific workflows, ensures data interoperability, and aligns vertical features with clinical and operational requirements.  

## Receives From  
| Builder        | What                  | Format      |  
|----------------|-----------------------|-------------|  
| Data Engineer  | Patient data streams  | CSV/JSON    |  
| Compliance Officer | Regulatory guidelines | PDF/JSON  |  
| UI/UX Designer | Interface mockups     | Figma/Sketch|  

## Produces For  
| Builder        | What                        | Format      |  
|----------------|-----------------------------|-------------|  
| Data Scientist | Anonymized datasets         | Parquet     |  
| Operations Manager | Workflow automation specs | YAML        |  
| Compliance Officer | Compliance-ready interface | PDF       |  

## Boundary  
Does NOT handle audit trails, legal documentation, or end-to-end compliance validation (handled by Compliance Officer). Excludes case study development (handled by Research Team).
