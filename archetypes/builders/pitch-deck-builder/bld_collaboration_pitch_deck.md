## Crew Role  
Structures and populates pitch deck slides with core messaging, visuals, and data, aligning with strategic goals.  

## Receives From  
| Builder | What | Format |  
|---|---|---|  
| Strategist | Core message & value proposition | Text outline |  
| Designer | Visual assets (logos, charts) | Image files |  
| Data Analyst | Key metrics & stats | CSV/Excel |  

## Produces For  
| Builder | What | Format |  
|---|---|---|  
| Presenter | Structured pitch deck draft | Slide deck (PPT/XLSX) |  
| Designer | Placeholder-embedded slides | Slide deck with notes |  
| Strategist | Feedback on message clarity | Text comments |  

## Boundary  
Does NOT handle case studies (narrative team), pricing details (pricing team), or legal compliance (legal team).
