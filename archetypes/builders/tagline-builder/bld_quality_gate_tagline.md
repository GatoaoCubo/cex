# Quality Gate: Tagline Builder

## HARD gates (must pass or artifact is rejected)
- H01: Frontmatter has id, kind, title, version, created, quality:null
- H02: At least 5 variants across 3+ approaches
- H03: Recommended tagline has reasoning
- H04: No variant exceeds 15 words
- H05: Recommended tagline passes competitor-swap test (stated explicitly)

## SOFT gates (warnings, not blockers)
- S01: Short/medium/long variants present (all 3 lengths)
- S02: Context adaptations for at least 3 contexts
- S03: USP extracted and stated
- S04: Language consistent throughout (no PT/EN mixing unless bilingual brand)
- S05: Score breakdown present for top variants
- S06: Emotional + functional approaches both represented

## Scoring Rubric
| Dimension | Weight | 10/10 means |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Memorability | 25% | Recalled unprompted after 24h |
| Differentiation | 25% | Could NOT be used by any competitor |
| Clarity | 20% | Understood in 3 seconds (billboard test) |
| Emotional Impact | 15% | Triggers specific feeling in target audience |
| Versatility | 15% | Works across hero, social, email, ad, pitch |
