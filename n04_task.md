# Churn Prevention Playbook

## Signal Detection
- **Engagement drop**: 30%+ decline in app usage
- **Feedback patterns**: 2+ negative sentiment signals in support tickets
- **Behavioral shifts**: 100% decrease in feature adoption
- **Payment anomalies**: 2+ failed payments in 30 days
- **Silence**: 90+ days without activity

## Intervention Triggers
1. **Automated alerts** (triggered by signal detection)
2. **Manual review** (for high-value accounts)
3. **Proactive outreach** (before churn confirmation)
4. **Escalation protocols** (for critical accounts)

## Save-the-Account Scripts
### Tier 1: Early Warning
- "We've noticed [specific signal] - would you like to discuss solutions?"
- "Your account activity suggests [issue] - our team can help resolve this"

### Tier 2: Active Intervention
- "We're seeing [specific pattern] - here's how we can prevent account loss"
- "Let's schedule a call to address [specific issue] and improve your experience"

### Tier 3: Crisis Management
- "We need to resolve [specific issue] immediately to retain your account"
- "Our team is prioritizing your account - let's resolve [specific problem] today"

## Metrics Tracking
- Churn rate reduction
- Customer satisfaction score
- Net promoter score
- Account reactivation rate
- Time-to-intervention metrics
