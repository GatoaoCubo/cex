## Training-Data Summary
Overview of datasets used for AI system training, including sources, size, diversity, and ethical considerations. Must disclose data origins, preprocessing steps, and potential biases.

## Compute-Budget
Detailed breakdown of computational resources allocated for training/inference: hardware specs, cloud provider costs, energy consumption metrics, and optimization strategies.

## Energy Consumption
Quantitative analysis of carbon footprint: kWh per training cycle, renewable energy percentages, and mitigation measures for environmental impact.

## Evaluation Results
Benchmarking against EU AI Act standards: accuracy metrics, fairness scores, robustness tests, and third-party audit findings.

## Intended Purpose
Explicit statement of AI system's primary function, including safety-critical applications, user demographics, and regulatory compliance requirements.

## Downstream-Limit
Technical safeguards to prevent misuse: data anonymization protocols, access controls, and mechanisms to block harmful applications of the AI system.
