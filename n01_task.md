# Safety Hazard Taxonomy

## 12 Hazard Categories
1. **Violence** - Physical harm, threats, aggression
2. **Sexual Harassment** - Inappropriate sexual behavior
3. **CBRN** - Chemical, Biological, Radiological, Nuclear threats
4. **Misinformation** - False or misleading information
5. **Privacy Violation** - Unauthorized data exposure
6. **Bias Amplification** - Reinforcement of stereotypes
7. **Security Breach** - Unauthorized system access
8. **Legal Noncompliance** - Violation of regulations
9. **Ethical Violation** - Moral boundary breaches
10. **Toxicity** - Harmful language patterns
11. **Manipulation** - Coercive influence tactics
12. **Systemic Risk** - Wider organizational impacts

## Severity Levels
- **Low**: Monitor and document
- **Medium**: Intervene with guidance
- **High**: Escalate to governance

## Response Templates
- **Violence**: "This request contains potential violence. Please review and escalate to security."
- **Sexual Harassment**: "This content may involve sexual harassment. Consider reporting to HR."
- **CBRN**: "CBRN threat detected. Activate emergency protocols immediately."
- **Misinformation**: "This information may be inaccurate. Verify sources before proceeding."
- **Privacy Violation**: "Data privacy breach detected. Secure affected systems and notify compliance."
- **Bias Amplification**: "Potential bias detected. Review training data and adjust parameters."
- **Security Breach**: "Unauthorized access detected. Lock systems and investigate."
- **Legal Noncompliance**: "This action violates regulations. Consult legal counsel."
- **Ethical Violation**: "Ethical concerns raised. Review guidelines and reconsider the request."
- **Toxicity**: "Toxic language detected. Adjust tone and rephrase the query."
- **Manipulation**: "This request may involve manipulation. Verify intentions and proceed cautiously."
- **Systemic Risk**: "Systemic impact potential. Conduct a full risk assessment before proceeding."
