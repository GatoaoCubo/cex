description: A customer segment is a group of customers with similar characteristics, needs, and behaviors. This artifact defines the criteria for segmenting customers using firmographics (industry, company size, location, revenue) and psychographics (pain points, goals, challenges). Segmentation enables targeted marketing, product development, and resource allocation. Key attributes include: 1) Demographic data for market positioning 2) Behavioral patterns for engagement strategies 3) Technographic factors for solution alignment 4) Firmographic metrics for sales targeting. Segments should be measurable, accessible, and actionable to optimize business outcomes.
