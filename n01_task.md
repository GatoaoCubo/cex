description: |
  Customer segmentation defines distinct groups of customers based on shared characteristics. Key firmographics include:
  - Industry (e.g., SaaS, manufacturing)
  - Company size (employee count, revenue range)
  - Geographic location (region, country)
  - Technological maturity (digital adoption level)
  - Buying center structure (decision-makers, influencers)

  Segmentation also identifies customer needs through:
  - Pain points (operational challenges, cost inefficiencies)
  - Goals (growth objectives, market expansion)
  - Decision-making processes (purchase criteria, approval hierarchies)
  - Usage patterns (product adoption stage, frequency of use)

  This artifact establishes clear criteria for segmenting audiences, enabling targeted marketing, personalized product development, and optimized sales strategies. Segments should be actionable, measurable, and aligned with business objectives.
