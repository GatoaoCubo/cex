Customer segment definition is a structured artifact containing:

1. **Firmographics** (company characteristics):
   - Industry vertical (e.g., SaaS, healthcare)
   - Company size (employee count, revenue range)
   - Geographic location (country, region, timezone)
   - Technology stack (cloud providers, infrastructure)
   - Business model (B2B, B2C, subscription, freemium)

2. **Needs Analysis** (pain points and goals):
   - Core challenges (e.g., customer acquisition, retention)
   - Decision-making criteria (budget, ROI metrics)
   - Buying process stages (awareness, consideration, decision)
   - Key stakeholders (technical buyers, C-suite)

3. **Behavioral Insights**:
   - Purchase frequency and lifecycle stage
   - Preferred communication channels
   - Technology adoption maturity
   - Competitive landscape positioning

4. **Segmentation Criteria**:
   - Demographic filters
   - Psychographic profiles
   - Geographic clustering
   - Behavioral patterns
   - Technographic attributes

This definition enables precise targeting through structured data about target audiences, guiding sales strategies and marketing campaigns.
