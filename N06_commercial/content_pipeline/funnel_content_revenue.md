# Content-to-Revenue Funnel Map

> Every piece of content GATO3 publishes has a job. This map assigns that job.
> 90 posts Instagram + 26 blog posts over 90 days. Zero waste.

---

## 1. Funnel Architecture

```
AWARENESS (30%)          CONSIDERATION (40%)      CONVERSION (20%)        RETENTION (10%)
~27 IG + ~5 blog         ~36 IG + ~12 blog        ~18 IG + ~6 blog        ~9 IG + ~3 blog
                                                                           
Goal: REACH              Goal: TRUST              Goal: PURCHASE          Goal: REPEAT
Metric: impressions,     Metric: saves,           Metric: clicks,         Metric: reorder,
shares, new followers    profile visits,          add-to-cart,            NPS, UGC
                         blog time-on-page        checkout starts         submissions
```

## 2. Stage 1: AWARENESS (30% of content = ~27 IG posts + ~5 blog posts)

### Content Types

| Content Type | Format | Frequency | Goal |
|-------------|--------|-----------|------|
| Memes (relatable humor) | Short Reel 7-15s | 2x/week | Shares, discovery |
| Trending audio Reels | Reel 7-15s | 1x/week | Algorithm reach |
| "Voce sabia?" cat facts | Carousel 5-7 slides | 1x/week | Saves + shares |
| Seasonal awareness hooks | Carousel or Reel | As calendar dictates | Topical relevance |
| Cat meme + educational twist | Reel or static + caption | 1x/week | "Humor educativo" positioning |

### Engagement Targets

| Metric | Target | Benchmark Source |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Reach per post | 3-5x follower count (Reels) | Social Insider 2026 |
| Share rate | 2-4% of reach | Cat account averages |
| New followers per week | 150-300 (Month 1), 300-600 (Month 3) | Micro-account growth curve |
| Engagement rate | 3-5% | Nano/micro benchmark (10K-100K) |

### CTA Rules (Awareness Stage)

| CTA Type | When | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| Follow | Every awareness post | "Siga @gatoaocubo3 para mais dicas felinas" |
| Share | Humor/meme content | "Marca aquele amigo gateiro" |
| Save | Educational facts | "Salva pra consultar depois" |
| NEVER: link in bio | Awareness posts | Product links kill sharability at this stage |

### Product Mention Rules (Awareness)

- **ZERO direct product mentions** in awareness content
- Brand is implicit through visual identity (PB minimalista, Allrounder/Kenao fonts)
- Watermark on Reels: small @gatoaocubo3 only
- Exception: packaging appears in unboxing UGC (organic, not placed)

### UTM Convention (Awareness)

Not applicable -- awareness content drives follows, not clicks. Track via Instagram Insights follower source.

## 3. Stage 2: CONSIDERATION (40% of content = ~36 IG posts + ~12 blog posts)

### Content Types

| Content Type | Format | Frequency | Goal |
|-------------|--------|-----------|------|
| Educational carousels (behavior) | Carousel 8-10 slides | 2x/week | Saves, authority |
| Blog how-to guides | Blog 1,500-2,500 words | 2x/week | SEO, trust |
| "Por que" explainers | Carousel + Blog companion | 1x/week | Deep trust |
| Problem-solution posts | Reel 30-60s | 1x/week | Demonstrate expertise |
| Ro protocol guides | Blog + carousel summary | 2x/month | Persona authority |

### Content-to-Product Category Mapping (Consideration)

| Content Topic | Educational Hook (80%) | Natural Product Bridge (20%) | Product Category |
|--------------|----------------------|---------------------------|-----------------|
| "Por que gatos dormem tanto" | Sleep science, polyphasic cycles | "Uma boa cama faz diferenca" | Camas |
| "Seu gato nao arranha por vinganca" | Scent glands, territory marking | "Arranhador no lugar certo" | Arranhadores |
| "Gatos nao suam: a ciencia" | Thermoregulation mechanisms | "Superficies frias ajudam" | Tapete gelado |
| "A sequencia de caca do gato" | Hunt sequence (eye-stalk-chase-pounce-kill) | "Brinquedos que completam o ciclo" | Brinquedos |
| "Seu gato NAO bebe agua suficiente" | Desert ancestor, CKD prevention | "Fontes aumentam consumo 50%" | Fonte de agua |
| "NUNCA coloque agua perto da comida" | Instinct: prey contaminates water | Placement guide | Comedouros |
| "Casa a prova de gato: checklist" | Safety audit, common dangers | "Tela de protecao em janelas" | Seguranca |

### Engagement Targets

| Metric | Target | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| Save rate | 4-8% of reach | Educational carousels drive saves |
| Profile visit rate | 5-10% of post engagers | Trust -> curiosity -> profile |
| Blog time-on-page | 3+ minutes | Deep content retains attention |
| Blog bounce rate | < 60% | Quality content keeps readers exploring |
| Link-in-bio clicks | 2-4% of post engagers | Bridge to site |

### CTA Rules (Consideration Stage)

| CTA Type | When | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| Visit blog | Educational carousels | "Guia completo no blog -- link na bio" |
| Save for later | Protocol/checklist posts | "Salva esse protocolo da Ro" |
| Comment question | Problem-solution posts | "Qual duvida voce tem sobre isso?" |
| Link in bio | Blog promo posts | "Link na bio: guia completo de hidratacao felina" |
| NEVER: "compre agora" | Educational content | Selling here breaks trust |

### Product Mention Rules (Consideration)

- Product appears in the **last 20% of content only** (80/20 rule from N01 research)
- Present as **one solution among many** -- "uma das formas de resolver..."
- NEVER claim product is the only solution
- Blog posts may include a product section AFTER the educational content
- Carousel final slide: soft product mention is acceptable

### UTM Convention (Consideration)

```
Blog to site: utm_source=blog&utm_medium=article&utm_campaign={topic_slug}&utm_content={article_slug}
IG to blog:   utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=linkinbio&utm_campaign=educativo_{week_number}
```

## 4. Stage 3: CONVERSION (20% of content = ~18 IG posts + ~6 blog posts)

### Content Types

| Content Type | Format | Frequency | Goal |
|-------------|--------|-----------|------|
| Product showcase (contextual) | Carousel 5-7 slides | 1x/week | Click-through |
| Unboxing/review | Reel 30-60s | 2x/month | Authentic demo |
| Comparison/guide | Blog 1,500+ words | 2x/month | SEO purchase intent |
| Customer testimonial/UGC | Reel or carousel | 2x/month | Social proof |
| Bundle feature | Carousel | 1x/month | AOV increase |

### Product Showcase Rules

| Rule | Why | Example |
|------|-----|---------|
| Contextualize, never isolate | Product in use > product on white background | Gato dormindo no Donut > Donut on shelf |
| Show the problem first | Establishes need before solution | "Gato arranhando sofa" -> "Arranhador resolveu" |
| Include price only on site | IG content is desire-building, not price-checking | "Confira no site" not "R$599" |
| Feature real cats | Authenticity > studio shots | UGC or team cats preferred |
| Max 1 direct product post per week | Avoids brand fatigue | Intersperse with education |

### Engagement Targets

| Metric | Target | Benchmark |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| CTR to site (from IG) | 1-2% of post reach | Instagram pet brand average |
| CTR to site (from blog) | 3-5% of article readers | Blog CTA benchmark |
| Add-to-cart rate | 8-12% of site visitors from content | E-commerce benchmark |
| Conversion rate (cart to purchase) | 2-3% of site visitors | Brazilian pet e-commerce avg |

### CTA Rules (Conversion Stage)

| CTA Type | When | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| Link in bio | Every product post | "Link na bio -- curadoria GATO3" |
| Story swipe-up (if eligible) | Product features | Direct product link |
| Blog internal link | Product-adjacent articles | Contextual link to product page |
| Bundle suggestion | After single product interest | "Combine com..." |
| NEVER: urgency fake | Always | No "ultimas unidades!" if not true |

### UTM Convention (Conversion)

```
IG feed to site:  utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=produto_{product_slug}&utm_content={post_id}
IG reel to site:  utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=reel&utm_campaign=showcase_{product_slug}
Blog to product:  utm_source=blog&utm_medium=article&utm_campaign=review_{product_slug}&utm_content=cta_inline
IG Story to site: utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=story&utm_campaign=produto_{product_slug}
```

## 5. Stage 4: RETENTION (10% of content = ~9 IG posts + ~3 blog posts)

### Content Types

| Content Type | Format | Frequency | Goal |
|-------------|--------|-----------|------|
| Customer usage tips | Carousel 5 slides | 2x/month | Increase product satisfaction |
| UGC reposts (#MeuGato3) | Reel or carousel | 2x/month | Community, social proof |
| Loyalty/exclusive offers | Stories | 1x/month | Repeat purchase |
| "Did you know?" post-purchase | Blog | 1x/month | Reduce returns, increase usage |
| B2B partner spotlight | Carousel | 1x/month | Channel partner engagement |

### Engagement Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| UGC submissions per month | 10+ (Month 1), 30+ (Month 3) |
| Repeat purchase rate | 15-20% within 90 days |
| Customer photo/video rate | 5% of purchasers share content |
| NPS (when measured) | 50+ |

### CTA Rules (Retention)

| CTA Type | When | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| UGC invite | Every purchase email | "Compartilhe com #MeuGato3" |
| Review request | 14 days post-purchase | "Conte como foi a experiencia" |
| Reorder nudge | Consumable products (30-60 days) | "Hora de repor?" |
| Referral | Happy customers | "Indique e ganhe desconto" |

### UTM Convention (Retention)


## 8. Cross-Channel Funnel Integration

```
Instagram                    Blog                        Site (gato3.com.br)          Marketplace
(discovery + community)      (SEO + deep trust)          (conversion + catalog)       (incremental reach)
                                                                                      
Awareness Reels -->          Blog companion post -->      Product page visit -->       Mercado Livre listing
Consideration carousel -->   Full guide -->               Add to cart -->              Shopee listing
Conversion product post -->  Review article -->           Checkout -->                 (attribution harder)
Retention UGC -->            Usage guide -->              Reorder -->

UTM: instagram/reel          UTM: blog/organic             (GA4 events)                (native dashboards)
```

### Marketplace Attribution Note

Mercado Livre and Shopee have limited attribution capabilities. GATO3 should:
1. Include a card in every package: "Siga @gatoaocubo3 para dicas felinas"
2. Track marketplace orders separately in Supabase
3. Use marketplace-specific promotional codes when running sales
4. Accept that ~30-40% of marketplace revenue may be content-influenced but not directly attributable

## 9. Content ROI by Funnel Stage

| Stage | Content Investment (hours/week) | Expected Revenue Contribution | ROI Timeline |
|-------|-------------------------------|------------------------------|-------------|
| Awareness | 4h (memes, Reels, trending) | Indirect (follower growth feeds funnel) | 60-90 days |
| Consideration | 8h (blog + carousels + research) | Indirect (trust feeds conversion) | 30-60 days |
| Conversion | 4h (product posts, UGC curation) | Direct (R$2,240-8,400/month by Month 3) | 14-30 days |
| Retention | 2h (UGC, tips, community) | Direct (repeat revenue +15-20%) | 30-90 days |
| **Total** | **18h/week** | **R$2,640-10,400/month by Month 3** | Progressive |

The 18h/week investment in content creation is the single highest-ROI activity GATO3 can perform in the first 90 days. Paid ads amplify this but are not required for initial traction.
