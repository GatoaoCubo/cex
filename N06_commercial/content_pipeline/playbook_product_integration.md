# Product Integration Playbook

> Golden rule: educate first, present product as natural conclusion. Never the reverse.
> Source: N01 kc_product_content_bridges.md (80/20 rule validated across all product categories).

---

## 1. The 80/20 Integration Framework

```
  CONTENT STRUCTURE (every product-adjacent post)

  |----- 80% EDUCATION -----|--- 20% PRODUCT --|
  
  Science/data/practical tip   "Uma das formas
  that stands on its own       de resolver isso e..."
  
  RULE: If you remove the product mention,
        the content must STILL be valuable.
```

### Never-Do List

| Action | Why | Alternative |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| Start post with product name | Triggers "ad" mental filter | Start with the problem or fact |
| Product-only carousels (catalog style) | Boring, low engagement, brand damage | Contextualize: cat using product |
| "Compre agora" / "Adquira ja" | Sounds like infomercial, breaks brand voice | "Conheca" / "Descubra" / "Confira" |
| Fake urgency ("ultimas unidades!") | Erodes trust if repeated | Real urgency only (Black Friday, actual stock limits) |
| Claim product is the only solution | Defensive, arrogant | "Uma das formas..." acknowledges alternatives |
| Compare GATO3 to competitor by name | Brand policy: never name competitors | Compare product TYPES, not brands |
| Price in Instagram feed posts | Reduces shareability, looks like classified ad | Price on site only; IG builds desire |
| Multiple products in one educational post | Confuses and dilutes | One product bridge per educational piece |

## 2. Integration Intensity Scale

| Level | Definition | When to Use | Frequency Cap |
|-------|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| **L0 -- Invisible** | Brand present only through visual identity (fonts, colors, watermark) | Awareness content (memes, trending Reels) | No cap |
| **L1 -- Whisper** | Product category mentioned generically ("camas elevadas ajudam") | Educational carousels, blog intros | 3x/week max |
| **L2 -- Mention** | Product appears by name in final 20% of content | Consideration content (how-tos, guides) | 2x/week max |
| **L3 -- Feature** | Product is the subject but framed by education | Conversion content (reviews, comparisons) | 1x/week max |
| **L4 -- Showcase** | Direct product post with CTA to site | Product launches, seasonal campaigns | 1x/week max |

### Weekly Budget by Level

| Week Type | L0 | L1 | L2 | L3 | L4 | Total Posts |
|-----------|----|----|----|----|----|----|
| Normal week | 2 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 0 | 5 IG |
| Product launch week | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 1 | 5 IG |
| Campaign week (Black Friday, Dia do Gato) | 1 | 0 | 1 | 1 | 2 | 5 IG |

## 3. Per-Product Integration Guide

### 3.1 Donut GATO3 (Cama Tunel 3 em 1) -- R$ 599

**Hero product. Highest price = most touchpoints needed before conversion.**

| Dimension | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| Natural content connections | Sleep science, cat thermoregulation, senior cat comfort, apartment living, winter care |
| Best formats | Blog deep review, "30 dias com o Donut" series (Reel), carousel (3 modos de usar), UGC |
| Ideal frequency | Max 1 direct mention per week; indirect references (sleep content) unlimited |
| Soft CTA | "Saiba mais sobre camas que acompanham o sono do seu gato" |
| Hard CTA | "Conheca o Donut GATO3 no site -- link na bio" (max 2x/month) |
| Conversion path | Blog review -> carousel summary -> UGC social proof -> site visit -> purchase |
| Seasonal hooks | Inverno (conforto), Dia das Maes (presente premium), Black Friday (desconto raro) |
| Meme integration | "R$599 na cama e o gato dorme na caixa" (self-deprecating humor builds authenticity) |

|-----------|------|

## 4. Blog Product Integration Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Product section placement | ALWAYS after the educational body. Never in the intro. |
| Internal link format | Contextual anchor text: "cama elevada para gatos" links to product page, not "clique aqui" |
| Max products per article | 3 (1 primary + 2 complementary) |
| Affiliate-style disclosure | Not legally required (own products) but transparency builds trust: "Produtos GATO3 selecionados para este tema" |
| Cross-link to other articles | Every product article links to 2-3 related educational articles |
| SEO consideration | Product name in H2, not H1. H1 = educational topic. |

### Blog Article Structure (product-adjacent)

```
H1: Educational topic ("Por que gatos arranham -- a ciencia completa")
  Intro: problem statement + hook
  H2: Science section 1 (anatomy, studies)
  H2: Science section 2 (behavior, psychology)
  H2: Practical tips (placement, training)
  H2: "Produtos que ajudam" (THIS IS THE 20%)
    - Primary product with contextual description
    - 1-2 complementary products
    - Link to product pages with UTM
  H2: Conclusion + Ro's protocol summary
  CTA: "Salve este guia" + link to related articles
```

## 5. Instagram Stories Product Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Product polls | "Seu gato prefere: cama aberta ou toca?" (engagement + product awareness) |
| Swipe-up/link sticker | Only on L3-L4 content. Never on educational stories. |

| Countdown sticker | Only for real launches/sales. Never for evergreen products. |

## 7. Voice Calibration for Product Mentions

| Situation | Do (sofisticado-acolhedor) | Don't (generic pet shop) |
|-----------|--------------------------|-------------------------|
| Introducing product | "Estudos mostram que fontes aumentam consumo em 50%" | "Compre nossa fonte!" |
| Comparing options | "Sisal vs carpete: a maioria dos gatos prefere sisal" | "Nosso arranhador de sisal e o melhor" |
| Handling objections | "DIY tambem funciona. Para quem busca praticidade..." | "Nao perca tempo com gambiarras" |
| Price justification | "Curadoria veterinaria + design minimalista" | "Preco imperdivel!" |
| Urgency | "Inverno comeca em 3 semanas -- seu gato esta preparado?" | "ULTIMAS UNIDADES!!!" |
| Social proof | "Dona Maria, tutora de 3 persas, conta..." | "Milhares de clientes satisfeitos!" |
