# Promotional Calendar Overlay

> Promotions are a scalpel, not a machete. Every sale has a reason, a duration, and a brand-safe format.
> Source: N01 kc_seasonal_calendar_pet.md for date alignment, brand_config.yaml for voice rules.

## 1. Promotional Philosophy (GATO3)

### Core Principles

| Principle | Rule | Why |
|-----------|------|-----|
| Education before promotion | Never launch a promo without prior educational content on the topic | Promo feels earned, not random |
| Real urgency only | Discount only when there's a real reason (seasonal, inventory, date) | Fake urgency erodes premium positioning |
| Brand voice survives promos | "Sofisticado-acolhedor" tone applies even during sales | Screaming "SALE!!!" is for pet shops, not GATO3 |
| Frequency cap | Max 1 promo per month (4-5 per quarter) | Scarcity protects perceived value |
| Bundle > discount | Prefer "kit com desconto" over "X% off individual item" | Bundles increase AOV; discounts train price sensitivity |
| Never discount Donut below 10% | Premium product at R$599 -- deep discounts destroy positioning | 5-10% max, or bundle with accessories |

### What GATO3 Never Does

| Action | Why | Alternative |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| Permanent discount section on site | Trains visitors to wait for sales | "Curadoria da Semana" (rotating highlight, full price) |
| "LIQUIDACAO" / "SALE" in all caps | Breaks brand voice, looks like Shopee marketplace | "Oferta especial" or "Kit com beneficio" |
| Daily deal / flash sale rotation | Creates transactional relationship, not brand loyalty | Monthly events with clear start/end |
| Discount without educational context | Product feels cheap, not valued | "Porque hidratacao importa... e nesta semana, a fonte tem frete gratis" |
| Competitor price matching | Race to bottom, impossible to sustain | Compete on education + curation quality |

## 3. Promo Content Format Rules

### How to Create Promo Content That Doesn't Break Brand Voice

#### Format: Educational Lead-In

```
Week -2: Educational content on the topic (NO mention of sale)
  Example: "5 sinais de que seu gato precisa de uma cama melhor" (carousel)

Week -1: Deeper education + soft product mention
  Example: Blog: "Ciencia do sono felino: o que a pesquisa diz sobre camas"

Promo Day: Sale announcement with educational context
  Example: "Porque sono importa tanto quanto alimentacao...
            esta semana, camas GATO3 com 10% off + frete gratis"

Week +1: Post-sale content (UGC from buyers, usage tips)
  Example: "3 tutores que mudaram a rotina de sono do gato" (customer stories)
```

#### Template: Promo Carousel (5 slides)

| Slide | Content | Visual |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 1 | Educational hook (problem/fact) | Clean, educational graphic |
| 2 | Science/data supporting the need | Infographic or data point |
| 3 | How GATO3 product addresses the need | Product in context (cat using) |
| 4 | Promo details (dates, discount, conditions) | Minimalist design, PB palette |
| 5 | CTA + link in bio | Clear, simple, "Ate {date}" |

#### Template: Promo Reel (15-30s)

```
0-5s:  Hook (problem or relatable moment)
5-15s: Educational context (why this matters)
15-20s: Product reveal (natural usage)
20-25s: Promo overlay text (discount + dates)
25-30s: CTA ("Link na bio -- valido ate {date}")
```

#### Template: Promo Blog Post Structure

```
H1: Educational title (NOT "SALE" or promo in title)
  Example: "Guia completo: como escolher a cama ideal para seu gato"

Body: Full educational content (80%)
  - Science
  - Practical tips
  - Comparison criteria

H2: "Oferta GATO3 desta semana" (20%)
  - Product highlight
  - Promo details
  - Link to product page with UTM
  - Validity dates

Footer: "Este guia faz parte da curadoria GATO3. Saiba mais em gato3.com.br"
```

### Visual Identity During Promos

| Element | Normal Content | During Promo |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| Color palette | PB minimalista (black, white, gray) | PB + ONE accent color per campaign |
| Typography | Allrounder headings, Kenao body | Same -- never change fonts for promos |
| Tone | Sofisticado-acolhedor | Same + slight warmth increase (max VOICE_WARMTH: 4.5) |
| Emoji use | Minimal (brand rule) | Zero increase -- no fire emoji, no siren |
| Price display | Never on IG feed | Permitted in promo carousel slide 4 only |
| "SALE" / "PROMO" text | Never | "Oferta especial" or "Kit com beneficio" |

## 4. Discount Strategy

### Discount Tiers

| Tier | When | Discount | Example |
|------|------|----------|---------|
| **Frete gratis** | Launch, low-value threshold (R$99+) | Shipping cost absorbed | "Frete gratis para ABC Paulista" |
| **Kit bundle** | Gift dates, cross-sell | Save R$20-50 vs individual | "Kit Gato Feliz: Cama + Arranhador + Brinquedo" |
| **5-10% off** | Seasonal, first purchase | Category-wide or individual | "10% off em camas -- codigo INVERNO10" |
| **15% off** | Dia do Gato (annual), loyalty | Full catalog | "15% off + frete gratis -- Mes do Gato" |
| **20% off** | Black Friday ONLY | Full catalog (tiered: 10/15/20 by cart value) | "Black Friday GATO3" |

### Discount Mechanics

| Mechanic | Use Case | Advantage |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Coupon code | IG-exclusive (`INSTAGRAM10`), blog-exclusive (`BLOG10`) | Attribution tracking |
| Automatic at checkout | Frete gratis for orders above threshold | Reduces friction |
| Bundle pricing | Kit at fixed price < sum of parts | Increases AOV, harder to compare |
| Tiered by cart value | R$99 = 10%, R$199 = 15%, R$399 = 20% | Incentivizes larger orders |

### Donut GATO3 Special Rules (R$599)

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Max discount | 10% (R$539 floor) |
| Never in clearance | Premium positioning must be protected |
| Bundle-only promos | "Donut + Escova + Brinquedo = R$649" (save R$79) better than "Donut R$539" |
| Payment plan highlight | "12x de R$49.92 sem juros" (reduce sticker shock, not price) |

## 5. B2B Content Adaptation

### What Content B2B Partners Can Use

| Content Type | Adaptation | How GATO3 Provides |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Product photos | High-res without GATO3 branding overlay | Shared Drive folder (updated monthly) |
| Educational carousels | Repost with "via @gatoaocubo3" credit | Repost permission policy |
| Product descriptions | Full copy with features/benefits | Product data sheet (PDF) |
| Pricing sheets | Wholesale pricing table | B2B portal or PDF (updated quarterly) |
| Promo materials | Co-branded sale assets | Provided 2 weeks before promo |

### B2B Promo Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Minimum advertised price (MAP) | B2B partners may NOT advertise below GATO3's retail price | 
| Promo alignment | Partners run promos ONLY during GATO3-approved windows |
| Co-branding | Partner logo + "Parceiro GATO3" badge. GATO3 visual identity preserved. |
| Discount transparency | Partners set their own margins above wholesale. GATO3 doesn't dictate retail price. |
| Content credit | All GATO3 educational content reposted by partners must tag @gatoaocubo3 |

### B2B Content Calendar Sync

| GATO3 Event | B2B Partner Receives | When |
|-------------|---------------------|------|
| Dia das Maes kits | Kit product shots + suggested social copy | May 1 (D-9) |
| Inverno campaign | Winter product photos + educational content | Jun 7 (D-14) |
| Dia do Gato | Full campaign kit (photos, copy, promo codes) | Jul 25 (D-14) |
| Black Friday | Promo structure + co-branded assets | Nov 1 (D-24) |

## 6. Promo Attribution (Coupon Code Strategy)

### Code Convention

```
{SOURCE}{DISCOUNT}{EVENT}
```

| Code | Source | Discount | Event |
|------|--------|----------|-------|
| `INSTAGRAM10` | Instagram | 10% | General (evergreen) |
| `BLOG10` | Blog | 10% | General |
| `WELCOME15` | Any (first purchase) | 15% | Onboarding |
| `DIADASMAES` | Any | Bundle price | Dia das Maes |
| `INVERNO10` | Any | 10% | Winter campaign |
| `GATO8` | Any | 15% | Dia do Gato (Aug 8) |
| `BF2026` | Any | Tiered | Black Friday |
| `AMIGO10` | Referral | 10% | Referral program |
| `B2B{PARTNER}` | B2B partner | Wholesale | B2B orders |

### Tracking Coupon Performance

| Metric | Tool | Frequency |
|--------|------|-----------|
| Uses per code | Shopify Reports | Weekly during promos |
| Revenue per code | Shopify Reports | Post-promo |
| Source validation | Cross-check UTM + coupon | Monthly |

## 10. Decision Tree: Should I Run a Promo?

```
Is there a calendar event within 14 days?
  YES --> Is it a MEGA event (Dia do Gato, Black Friday, Natal)?
            YES --> Full promo (15-20% off, 3-week prep, full campaign)
            NO  --> Is it a gift date (Maes, Namorados)?
                      YES --> Bundle promo (kits, 1-week prep)
                      NO  --> Seasonal promo (frete gratis or 10%, 1-week prep)
  NO  --> Do you have excess inventory in a specific category?
            YES --> Quiet bundle deal (no campaign, just site banner)
            NO  --> Do you need follower growth or email signups?
                      YES --> Lead magnet (free guide + email capture), NOT discount
                      NO  --> Don't run a promo. Invest in educational content instead.
```
