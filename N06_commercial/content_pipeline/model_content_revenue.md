## 8. Break-Even Analysis

### When does content pay for itself?

| Scenario | Monthly Cost | Break-even Orders | Break-even Month | Cumulative Deficit Before Break-even |
|----------|-------------|------------------|-----------------|-------------------------------------|
| Conservative (AOV R$50) | R$ 500 | 10 orders | Month 2-3 | R$ -875 |
| Moderate (AOV R$69) | R$ 640 | 9 orders | Month 2 | R$ -226 |
| Optimistic (AOV R$88) | R$ 800 | 9 orders | Month 1-2 | R$ +344 |

**Verdict**: Content pays for itself by Month 2 in all scenarios (excluding labor). The ROI accelerates as SEO compounds and follower base grows. By Month 6, content should be the highest-ROI channel.

## 10. Revenue Sensitivity Analysis

### What moves the needle most?

| Variable | +1 unit change | Revenue impact (Month 3) | Controllable? |
|----------|---------------|-------------------------|---------------|
| AOV (+R$10) | R$69 -> R$79 | +R$ 650/month (+14%) | Medium (bundle strategy) |
| Conversion rate (+0.5%) | 2.5% -> 3.0% | +R$ 897/month (+20%) | High (site UX, trust signals) |
| Blog traffic (+1,000) | 2,350 -> 3,350 | +R$ 690/month (+15%) | High (more articles, better SEO) |
| IG followers (+1,000) | 4,100 -> 5,100 | +R$ 540/month (+12%) | Medium (consistency) |
| IG CTR to site (+0.3%) | 1.2% -> 1.5% | +R$ 450/month (+10%) | Medium (CTA optimization) |

**Highest-leverage actions** (in order):
1. Optimize site conversion rate (trust badges, reviews, checkout UX)
2. Increase blog publishing cadence (3/week instead of 2)
3. Bundle strategy to increase AOV
4. Reel quality for discovery/follower growth
5. CTA A/B testing on Instagram posts
