# Government/GovTech Industry Vertical

## Core Standards
- **FedRAMP** (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program): Standardized security framework for cloud service providers serving U.S. federal agencies
- **FISMA** (Federal Information Security Modernization Act): Mandates security programs for federal agencies and contractors
- **GSA** (General Services Administration): Oversees federal procurement and IT modernization initiatives
- **CJIS** (CISA Joint Information Systems Security Program): Security requirements for systems handling sensitive federal data
- **Section 508**: Accessibility standards ensuring federal agencies' electronic and information technology is accessible to people with disabilities

## Use Cases
1. **Cloud Migration**: FedRAMP-compliant cloud providers enable secure federal data migration while meeting FISMA requirements
2. **Public Services**: GSA-led initiatives modernize government services with accessible digital platforms (Section 508 compliance)
3. **Cybersecurity**: CJIS standards protect sensitive law enforcement and intelligence data across federal networks
4. **Accessible Governance**: Section 508 guidelines ensure digital government services are usable by all citizens, including those with visual, auditory, or motor impairments
