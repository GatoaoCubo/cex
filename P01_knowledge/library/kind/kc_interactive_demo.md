# Interactive Product Demo Script

## Welcome (0:00-1:00)
1. **Step 1**: Greet user with product name and purpose  
   *Talk track*: "Welcome to [Product Name]! Today we'll walk through how this tool simplifies [specific task]"

2. **Step 2**: Show product interface  
   *Talk track*: "You'll notice our interface is designed for [key benefit] with [unique feature] at the core"

## Features Demo (1:00-4:00)
3. **Step 3**: Demonstrate core functionality  
   *Talk track*: "Let's see how [feature] works - simply [action], and our system automatically [outcome]"

4. **Step 4**: Show customization options  
   *Talk track*: "You can personalize this to fit your needs - from [option 1] to [option 2]"

## Benefits Visualization (4:00-6:00)
5. **Step 5**: Highlight time savings  
   *Talk track*: "This saves [X] hours weekly by [specific process] - let's quantify that"

6. **Step 6**: Show integration capabilities  
   *Talk track*: "Seamlessly connects with [tool A], [tool B], and [tool C] for a unified workflow"

## Call to Action (6:00-7:00)
7. **Step 7**: Provide next steps  
   *Talk track*: "Ready to implement? Start with [step 1], then [step 2] - our support team is here to help"
