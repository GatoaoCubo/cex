**Purpose**: Document technical requirements for high-risk AI systems under the EU AI Act (Article 53).

**Key Requirements**:
- **Training Data Summary**: Must disclose data sources, preprocessing methods, and representativeness.
- **Compute Budget**: Specify hardware specifications, cloud provider details, and energy consumption metrics (kWh).
- **Energy Consumption**: Provide lifecycle energy usage data with carbon footprint analysis.
- **Evaluation Results**: Include F1 scores, bias metrics, and performance benchmarks against baseline models.
- **Intended Purpose**: Clearly define the system's operational scope and use cases.
- **Downstream Limit**: Establish constraints on data processing, output formatting, and third-party integration.

**Compliance Notes**:
- Documentation must be in English and accessible to auditors.
- Technical details should demonstrate transparency and accountability.
- All metrics must be verifiable through third-party audits.
