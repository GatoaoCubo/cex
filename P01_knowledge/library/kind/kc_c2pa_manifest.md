**Overview**  
The C2PA 2.3 content credential manifest is a standardized format for proving authenticity of AI-generated media. It enables creators to assert ownership and transparency about AI-generated content.

**Key Components**  
1. **Claim**: A cryptographic assertion that the content was generated by a specific AI-ML model.  
2. **Assertions**: Metadata about the content's creation, including timestamps, input data sources, and generation parameters.  
3. **Ingredient**: A reference to the AI-ML model used, including its version and configuration.  
4. **Signature**: A cryptographic signature verifying the integrity of the manifest itself.  

**Purpose**  
This manifest ensures trust in AI-generated media by providing verifiable provenance, preventing misuse, and enabling content reuse with proper attribution.

**Usage**  
Embed the manifest in digital assets to enable verification tools to validate authenticity, track modifications, and ensure compliance with content usage policies.
