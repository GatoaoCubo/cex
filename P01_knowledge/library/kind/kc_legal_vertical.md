# Legal Industry Vertical: Key Concepts

## 1. Privilege
- **Attorney-Client Privilege**: Confidential communications between lawyer and client
- **Work Product Protection**: Shielded documents created for litigation preparation
- **Exceptions**: Spontaneous disclosures, third-party access, and non-privileged communications

## 2. Billable Hour
- **Time Tracking**: Detailed logging of attorney work (e.g., 1.5 hours for document review)
- **Billing Practices**: Fixed fees vs hourly rates, time entry validation
- **Ethical Considerations**: Prohibited billable hours for non-legal tasks

## 3. Contract Analysis
- **Key Elements**: Parties, obligations, termination clauses, governing law
- **Risk Assessment**: Identifying hidden liabilities and enforceability gaps
- **Negotiation Support**: Drafting clauses for dispute resolution and performance metrics

## 4. Use Cases
- **Document Review**: Privilege log creation and redaction
- **Compliance Audits**: Regulatory document analysis and risk mapping
- **Transaction Support**: Due diligence reviews and contract negotiation
- **Litigation Support**: Discovery document analysis and evidentiary review

This card provides foundational knowledge for legal vertical applications. For deeper analysis, consult specialized legal knowledge cards.
