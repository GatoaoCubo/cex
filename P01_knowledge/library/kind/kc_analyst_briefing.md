The Analyst Briefing is a strategic document used to position a product or service in the market, supported by data-driven proof points and real-world customer success stories. It typically includes:

- Market positioning and competitive analysis
- Key proof points from third-party research (Gartner, Forrester, IDC)
- Case studies and customer wins
- ROI metrics and use scenarios
- Strategic recommendations for stakeholders

This briefing helps align internal teams and external partners on the product's value proposition and market standing.
