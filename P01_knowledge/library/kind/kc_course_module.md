Purpose: A structured unit within an online course designed to deliver specific learning outcomes.
Structure: Includes instructional content, activities, and assessments to reinforce learning.
Learning Objectives: Clear, measurable goals that students aim to achieve by the end of the module.
Assessments: Tools to evaluate student understanding, such as quizzes, assignments, and projects.
Best Practices: Align objectives with assessments, use varied instructional methods, and provide feedback.
