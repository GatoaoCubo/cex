## Why Pet Businesses Choose PetDesk

### 1. Pet Profiles with Full History

Every pet gets a detailed profile: breed, allergies, vaccination dates, grooming preferences, and visit history. Staff see everything at a glance -- no more flipping through paper files or asking owners to repeat information.

- Vaccination and medication tracking with automatic expiry alerts
- Grooming notes carried across visits
- Photo timeline for each pet

### 2. Smart Scheduling and Automated Reminders

Fill your calendar without phone tag. Clients book online, receive SMS and email confirmations, and get reminders 24 hours before their appointment. No-shows drop by up to 40%.

- Online booking widget for your website
- Automated SMS/email reminders
- Waitlist management for peak hours

### 3. Revenue Dashboard and Client Insights

See your top services, busiest hours, and highest-value clients in real time. PetDesk turns your appointment data into actionable insights so you can stock smarter, staff better, and market where it counts.

- Monthly revenue and visit trends
- Client retention and lifetime value scores
- Product inventory alerts tied to service demand
