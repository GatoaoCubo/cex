# Instagram Content Plan

## 1. Post Concepts

### Concept 1 -- Carousel: "5 Things You Didn't Know About [Industry]"
- **Format**: Carousel (8 slides)
- **Hook slide**: Bold stat or contrarian statement
- **Middle slides**: One insight per slide, minimal text, branded colors
- **CTA slide**: "Save this for later" + follow prompt
- **Goal**: Educate + drive saves

### Concept 2 -- Reel: Behind-the-Scenes Process Reveal
- **Format**: Reel (30-45s)
- **Structure**: Trending audio + fast cuts showing real workflow
- **Text overlay**: Problem -> process -> result
- **CTA**: "Follow for more [niche] tips"
- **Goal**: Humanize brand + drive follows

### Concept 3 -- Static: Client Win / Testimonial Spotlight
- **Format**: Single image (branded quote card)
- **Design**: Client quote on brand-colored background, logo watermark
- **Caption**: Full story -- problem, solution, outcome
- **CTA**: Link in bio for case study
- **Goal**: Social proof + conversions

### Concept 4 -- Carousel: Step-by-Step Tutorial
- **Format**: Carousel (6 slides)
- **Slide 1**: "How to [achieve result] in [N] steps"
- **Slides 2-5**: One step per slide with visual example
- **Slide 6**: Recap + CTA ("DM us [keyword] for the full guide")
- **Goal**: Value delivery + DM engagement

### Concept 5 -- Reel: Myth vs. Reality in [Industry]
- **Format**: Reel (15-20s)
- **Structure**: Split screen or text flip -- myth on one side, reality on the other
- **Tone**: Confident, slightly provocative
- **CTA**: "Drop a [emoji] if you believed the myth"
- **Goal**: Engagement (comments + shares)

## 3. Brand Voice Guide (Instagram)

### Tone Pillars

| Pillar        | Description                                        | Example                                      |
|---------------|----------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Confident     | Speak with authority, not arrogance                | "Here's what actually works."                |
| Conversational| Write like talking to a smart friend               | "Real talk -- most people get this wrong."   |
| Action-biased | Every post moves the audience toward something     | "Try this today. Report back."               |
| Genuine       | Show real work, real results, real people           | "This took us 3 tries. Here's the final."   |

### Do / Don't

| Do                                      | Don't                                    |
|-----------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Use short sentences in captions         | Write walls of text with no line breaks  |
| Lead with the hook in line 1            | Bury the value after "more..."           |
| Use "you" and "your" frequently         | Talk about the brand in third person     |
| Include one clear CTA per post          | Stack multiple CTAs in one caption       |
| Match caption tone to format            | Use formal tone on a casual reel         |

### Caption Structure Template

```
[HOOK -- first line visible before "more"]

[VALUE -- 2-4 short paragraphs, line breaks between each]

[CTA -- one action, clear and specific]

[HASHTAGS -- in comment or after line breaks]
```

## 4. Hashtag Strategy

### Hashtag Tiers (use 15-20 per post, mixed from all tiers)

| Tier       | Volume        | Count per Post | Purpose                  | Examples                          |
|------------|---------------|----------------|--------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Broad      | 1M+ posts     | 2-3            | Discovery ceiling        | #marketing, #business, #growth   |
| Mid-range  | 100K-1M posts | 5-7            | Realistic ranking        | #contentstrategy, #brandtips     |
| Niche      | 10K-100K posts| 5-7            | High relevance, low noise| #instagramforbusiness, #reelstips|
| Branded    | Custom        | 1-2            | Community + tracking     | #YourBrandName, #YourBrandMethod |

### Hashtag Sets (rotate weekly to avoid shadowban patterns)

**Set A -- Educational posts**:
`#contenttips #marketingstrategy #socialmediatips #instagramgrowth #digitalmarketing #brandbuilding #contentcreator #growthhacks #marketingtips #businesstips #onlinebusiness #smallbusinesstips #entrepreneurtips #instagramtips #learnmarketing`

**Set B -- Behind-the-scenes / Reels**:
`#behindthescenes #dayinthelife #processreveal #creativeprocess #agencylife #workinprogress #bts #reelsinstagram #instagramreels #viralreels #trendingaudio #reelstips #contentcreation #videomarketing #shortformvideo`

**Set C -- Testimonials / Results**:
`#clientresults #testimonial #casestudy #resultsspeak #socialproof #beforeandafter #transformation #clientwin #successstory #realresults #proofofwork #happyclients #growthmindset #businessgrowth #resultsdriven`

### Hashtag Rules

1. Place hashtags in the **first comment**, not the caption body
2. Rotate sets -- never use the same 15 hashtags two posts in a row
3. Audit monthly -- remove any hashtag flagged or with declining reach
4. Track branded hashtag usage for community growth metrics
5. Research 3 new niche hashtags weekly to keep sets fresh
