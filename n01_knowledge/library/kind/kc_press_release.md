### Customization Guide
1. Replace bracketed text with specific details
2. Use bold for key terms in AP style
3. Maintain 50-70 word count for headline
4. Include 3-5 quotes from executives
5. Add 2-3 media contact options
6. Verify all facts and dates before publication
