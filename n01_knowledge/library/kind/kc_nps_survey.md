# NPS Survey Configuration

## Question
Standardized prompt: "On a scale from 0-10, how likely are you to recommend our service to a friend or colleague?"

## Scale
0-10 numeric scale with:
- 0-6: Detractors
- 7-8: Passives
- 9-10: Promoters

## Follow-up
- Detractors: Trigger 1:1 support call
- Promoters: Optional loyalty reward program enrollment
- Passives: Standard thank-you email

## Segmentation
- By customer tier (Basic/Pro/Enterprise)
- By service usage frequency
- By geographic region
- By product adoption stage

## Cadence
- Quarterly baseline survey
- Monthly for high-value accounts
- Triggered surveys for service incidents
- Annual net promoter score analysis

## Response Routing
- Automated email responses for scale
- CRM integration for account-specific follow-ups
- Analytics dashboard for real-time metrics
- Escalation to customer success team for complex cases
