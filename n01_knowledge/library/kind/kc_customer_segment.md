# Customer Segment Definition

Customer segmentation divides a customer base into distinct groups based on shared characteristics, needs, and behaviors. This artifact defines:

## Core Elements
1. **Firmographics**  
   - Industry type (e.g., SaaS, manufacturing)  
   - Company size (employee count, revenue range)  
   - Geographic location (country, region, city)  
   - Organizational structure (startup, enterprise)  

2. **Needs & Pain Points**  
   - Operational challenges (integration, compliance)  
   - Strategic goals (market expansion, cost reduction)  
   - Functional requirements (product features, service levels)  

3. **Behavioral Patterns**  
   - Purchase frequency and volume  
   - Product usage depth  
   - Customer lifecycle stage (new, active, dormant)  

This definition serves as the foundation for creating Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) and tailoring go-to-market strategies.
