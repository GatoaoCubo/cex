description: |
  Trajectory evaluation assesses an agent's sequence of decisions and actions across time, measuring effectiveness against goals. Key metrics include:

  1. **Success Rate**: Percentage of trajectories achieving target outcomes
  2. **Resource Efficiency**: Optimization of computational/energetic costs
  3. **Goal Alignment**: Degree of adherence to specified objectives
  4. **Robustness**: Performance under environmental perturbations
  5. **Safety Compliance**: Adherence to ethical/physical constraints

  Evaluation frameworks often combine reinforcement learning metrics with simulation environments. Trajectory analysis informs model refinement by identifying patterns in decision-making pathways, enabling iterative improvements in agent reliability and efficiency.
