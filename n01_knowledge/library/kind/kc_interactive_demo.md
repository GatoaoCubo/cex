# Interactive Product Demo Script

**Objective:** Guide users through product features with a structured, engaging tour

## Demo Structure
1. **Welcome Slide**  
   *Visual:* Product logo + tagline  
   *Talk Track:* "Welcome to [Product Name] - let's explore how it solves your challenges"

2. **Feature 1: Core Functionality**  
   *Visual:* Animated workflow diagram  
   *Talk Track:* "Our platform automates [specific task] with 95% accuracy"

3. **Feature 2: Customization Options**  
   *Visual:* Configuration panel screenshot  
   *Talk Track:* "Tailor your experience with 15+ customization settings"

4. **Feature 3: Integration Capabilities**  
   *Visual:* API connection diagram  
   *Talk Track:* "Seamlessly integrate with your existing tools ecosystem"

5. **Use Case Scenario**  
   *Visual:* Customer success story video (30s)  
   *Talk Track:* "See how [Customer] increased productivity by 40%"

6. **Call to Action**  
   *Visual:* CTA button + contact info  
   *Talk Track:* "Ready to transform your workflow? Let's discuss your requirements"

## Tour Guidelines
- Maintain 2-3 minute per section pacing
- Use conversational tone with technical depth
- Include live demos for complex features
- Prepare 2-3 follow-up questions per section
