# Education/EdTech Industry Vertical

## Key Regulations
- **FERPA**: Federal Educational Rights and Privacy Act protects student education records
- **COPPA**: Children's Online Privacy Protection Act governs data collection from minors

## Technical Standards
- **LTI (Learning Tools Interoperability)**: Enables seamless integration between LMS platforms and external educational tools

## Data Privacy Framework
- Student data privacy requires strict compliance with both FERPA and COPPA regulations
- Data encryption and access controls are critical for protecting sensitive information

## Use Cases
1. **LMS Integration**: Connect classroom management systems with third-party educational apps
2. **Data Analytics**: Analyze learning patterns while maintaining student privacy
3. **Content Delivery**: Distribute personalized learning materials through secure platforms
