description: |
  Customer segment definition is a strategic artifact that establishes clear boundaries for target markets. This knowledge card contains structured definitions of customer segments and Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) with firmographic data and behavioral insights.

  Firmographics include:
  - Industry vertical (e.g., SaaS, healthcare)
  - Company size (employee count, revenue range)
  - Geographic location (country, region, city)
  - Technological maturity (digital adoption stage)
  - Organizational structure (centralized/decentralized)

  Customer needs encompass:
  - Pain points (operational, financial, regulatory)
  - Decision-making criteria (budget, timeline, KPIs)
  - Buying behavior (purchase frequency, channel preferences)
  - Strategic goals (growth, efficiency, innovation)
  - Risk tolerance (conservative vs. aggressive)

  This artifact serves as the foundation for marketing strategies, product development, and sales targeting. It enables data-driven decisions by quantifying market opportunities and aligning offerings with specific customer profiles.
