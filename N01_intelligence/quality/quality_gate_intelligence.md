## Purpose
This quality gate ensures that all research outputs generated by the **N01 Intelligence Nucleus** (typically in the `Intelligence Brief` format) are accurate, reliable, structured, and actionable before they are propagated through the CEX system.

---

## Hard Gates
These are pass/fail criteria. Any "FAIL" result immediately rejects the artifact and triggers a correction loop.

| Gate ID | Description                                                                   | Check                                       |
|---------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| H01     | **Valid Structure**                                                           | Artifact body MUST contain `Key Judgements`, `Analysis`, and `Source Appendix` sections. |
| H02     | **Complete Citations**                                                        | Every claim or data point in the `Analysis` section MUST be followed by an inline citation, e.g., `[Source, Year]`. |
| H03     | **Non-Empty Appendix**                                                        | The `Source Appendix` section MUST NOT be empty and must list at least one source. |
| H04     | **No Placeholders**                                                           | Artifact MUST NOT contain placeholder text like `[TODO]`, `[add analysis here]`, or similar. |
| H05     | **Confidence Stated**                                                         | If the analysis contains any forward-looking statements or logical inferences, a `Confidence Level` (High, Medium, Low) MUST be stated. |

---

## Soft Gates (Scoring)
These criteria are scored to produce an overall quality metric. The total score determines if the artifact is accepted or flagged for review. A total score below **7.0** is considered a failure.

| Gate ID | Criterion                 | Weight | Description                                                                                                     |
|---------|---------------------------|--------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| S01     | **Clarity & Conciseness** | 30%    | Language is precise, unambiguous, and free of unnecessary jargon. Insights are communicated effectively.          |
| S02     | **Depth of Synthesis**    | 30%    | The analysis connects disparate facts to create novel insights, rather than just summarizing sources.           |
| S03     | **Source Quality**        | 20%    | The analysis prioritizes primary, peer-reviewed, and reputable sources over lower-quality commentary or blogs.  |
| S04     | **Actionability**         | 20%    | The `Key Judgements` are impactful and directly useful for strategic decision-making.                              |

---

## Actions
- **On Hard Gate FAIL**:
  - **Action**: `REJECT`
  - **Log**: Log the ID of the failed Hard Gate.
  - **Handoff**: Return the artifact to the N01 agent with a directive to correct the specific gate failure.
- **On Soft Gate Score < 7.0**:
  - **Action**: `ACCEPT_WITH_WARNING`
  - **Log**: Log the final score and flag the artifact for human review.
- **On Soft Gate Score >= 7.0**:
  - **Action**: `ACCEPT`
  - **Log**: Log the final score.
  - **Signal**: Propagate a `validation_complete` signal for the artifact.
