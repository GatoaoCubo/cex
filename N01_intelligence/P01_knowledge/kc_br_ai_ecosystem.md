## 7. Regulatory Environment

### PL 2338/2023 -- Brazilian AI Act (status as of April 2026)

| Stage | Date | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| Introduced | May 2023 | Senator Rodrigo Pacheco's proposal |
| Senate approved | December 10, 2024 | Full senate plenary vote |
| Forwarded to Chamber | March 17, 2025 | Chamber of Deputies review |
| Expected enactment | 2025-2026 | May include amendments |

Key provisions:
- Risk-based classification (mirrors EU AI Act structure)
- Pre-market conformity assessment for high-risk AI systems
- ANPD designated as competent authority (SIA -- National AI Governance System)
- Fines: up to R$50M or 2% of company turnover
- Human-centered development mandate
- Transparency requirements for AI-generated content

Key contrast with EU AI Act: Brazil's bill is more permissive on innovation; enforcement mechanism is lighter than EU's; ANPD already has precedent from LGPD enforcement.
