## Actionable Insights for GATO3

1. **3 MEGA campaigns per year**: Dia do Gato (Aug 8), Black Friday (Nov), Natal (Dec) -- these need dedicated planning and budget
2. **Safety content is non-negotiable**: Pascoa, Festa Junina, Reveillon -- publish BEFORE the events, not during
3. **Monthly themes create consistency**: Audience learns what to expect, increases retention
4. **Seasonal product ties are natural**: Winter = camas, Summer = tapete gelado, Year-round = fonte de agua
5. **UGC campaigns on special dates**: Dia do Gato + community hashtag = free content + engagement spike
6. **Content reuse**: Fireworks safety (Jun) can be adapted for Reveillon (Dec) and Carnaval (Feb)
7. **Lead times matter**: Major campaigns need 2-4 weeks of preparation. Mark in production calendar.
