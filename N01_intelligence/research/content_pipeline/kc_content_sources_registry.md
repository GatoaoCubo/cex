## 7. Source Reliability Tiers

| Tier | Score | Description | Usage Rule |
|------|-------|-------------|-----------|
| 1 (Authority) | 9-10/10 | Peer-reviewed, institutional, veterinary | Cite directly in content. "Segundo a Cornell..." |
| 2 (Professional) | 7-8/10 | Industry publications, verified data platforms | Reference for strategy; cite when data-backed |
| 3 (Inspiration) | 5-7/10 | Blogs, aggregators, media | Use for ideas and trends; verify claims before citing |
| 4 (Social) | 3-5/10 | Social media posts, unverified claims | Never cite as authority; use for trend detection only |

### Citation Rules for GATO3 Content

| Content Type | Minimum Citation Tier | Example |
|-------------|----------------------|---------|
| Health/safety posts | Tier 1 only | "Segundo a ASPCA, chocolate e toxico para gatos" |
| Behavior education | Tier 1-2 | "O protocolo Jackson Galaxy de introducao..." |
| Product claims | Tier 1-2 with study reference | "Estudo do PMC mostra aumento de 50% no consumo de agua" |
| Memes/humor | No citation needed | Pure entertainment |
| Seasonal tips | Tier 2-3 | General awareness, verified advice |
| Market data | Tier 2 | "Dados do Social Insider 2026 mostram..." |

## Actionable Insights for GATO3

1. **Tier 1 citations differentiate GATO3** -- no Brazilian pet brand cites Cornell, ISFM, or PMC. This is free authority.
2. **60+ sources give 6+ months of content angles** -- each source can generate 3-5 content pieces
3. **PT-BR sources are weaker on science** -- GATO3 fills this gap by translating Tier 1 EN sources into accessible PT-BR content
4. **Safety sources must be pre-loaded** -- before Pascoa, Festa Junina, Natal, have ASPCA/toxicity content ready
5. **Social Insider 2026 data is gold** -- use their benchmarks to set realistic engagement targets
6. **Jackson Galaxy is the content model** -- his approach (education + personality + product) is exactly what GATO3 should adapt for PT-BR
