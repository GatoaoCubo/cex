### Why Micro > Macro for GATO3 (Phase 1)

| Factor | Micro (10-50K) | Macro (500K+) |
|--------|---------------|---------------|
| Engagement rate | 2.71% avg | 0.61-0.68% |
| Cost per collab | Product gifting (~R$100-300) | R$2,000-10,000+ per post |
| Authenticity | High (feels like friend recommendation) | Medium (feels like ad) |
| Conversion | Higher (trust factor) | Lower (awareness > conversion) |
| GATO3 phase fit | Perfect for launch | Better for scale phase |

**Recommendation**: Start with 5-10 micro-influencers (cat-focused, PT-BR, 10-50K followers) via product gifting. Requires minimal budget, maximizes authentic endorsements.

## Actionable Insights for GATO3

1. **Character > brand**: Invest in Ro persona and featured cats, not just "GATO3" brand posts
2. **Micro-influencer network first**: 5-10 partnerships via product gifting = high ROI, authentic growth
3. **UGC from day 1**: #MeuGato3 hashtag, repost strategy, community building before scale
4. **Format mix follows data**: 60-70% Reels (discovery) + 20-30% Carousels (saves) + 10% Stories (engagement)
5. **5x/week is the sweet spot**: Consistent but not burnout-inducing, quality over volume
6. **Rescue feature is an engagement multiplier**: Monthly partnership with @ongmundogato or local NGOs
7. **Study @canseidesergato closely**: Largest Brazilian cat-only account. Character-driven format is proven
