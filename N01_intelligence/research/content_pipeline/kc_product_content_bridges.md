# Product-Content Bridges

> Target: 20% of GATO3 content mix (produto). The strategy: NEVER sell directly. Educate first, present product as the natural solution. This KC maps every GATO3 product category to science-backed educational content.

---

## 1. Bridge Architecture

```
SCIENCE FACT --> PROBLEM AWARENESS --> SOLUTION PRINCIPLE --> PRODUCT AS EXAMPLE
     |                   |                    |                       |
"Cats sleep 16h"  "Your cat needs    "Elevated, warm       "GATO3 cama
                   quality rest"       sleeping spots"       termica..."
```

**Rule**: The product appears ONLY in the last 20% of the content. The first 80% is pure education.

## 8. Content Bridge Rules (GATO3 Voice)

### The 80/20 Rule

- 80% of the content = pure education (science, data, practical tips)
- 20% = product mention (natural, not forced, always as "one solution")
- NEVER start a post with the product
- ALWAYS end educational content with "e uma das formas de resolver isso e..."

### Tone Calibration

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| "Estudos mostram que fontes de agua aumentam consumo em 50%" | "Compre nossa fonte de agua!" |
| "Gatos precisam completar a sequencia de caca" | "Nosso brinquedo e o melhor" |
| "Camas elevadas dao sensacao de seguranca" | "Somente a cama GATO3 resolve" |
| Present data first, product second | Lead with product, justify with data |
| Acknowledge alternatives ("DIY tambem funciona") | Claim product is the only solution |

### Competitor Comparison (NEVER Direct)

- NEVER mention competitor names in content
- Compare product TYPES, not brands
- Position through education depth, not claims
- Let quality speak through science, not superlatives

## Actionable Insights for GATO3

1. **Every product has a science story**: No product post should exist without an educational foundation
2. **Hunt sequence is the #1 content series opportunity**: Visual, shareable, multi-part, product-integrating
3. **Hydration content is evergreen AND seasonal**: Works year-round but especially in summer
4. **Scratching content is the highest-intent product bridge**: People actively searching for solutions = purchase-ready
5. **Seasonal product rotation keeps catalog fresh**: Match products to weather/calendar events naturally
6. **Bundle content drives AOV**: Educational bundles ("3 essenciais") outperform discount-driven bundles
7. **Never start with the product**: 80/20 rule protects brand trust and keeps content shareable
